Welcome to Books Collective Organization

"An investment in Knowledge Pays the Best Interest" - Benjamin Franklin

Here at Books Collective Organization (B.O.C.), we are aimed at distributing a variety of textbooks and reading materials to children and schools in zones experiencing a socioeconomic despression or a defecit in educational funding.

Accomplishments Thus Far

Back to the Top

Books Collective Organization (B.O.C.) may be relatively new but we have participated in several important events and made strides in our local city of Chicago, Illinois and surrounding states.Where can your school, library, or community group find free or low-cost books for kids? There are a number of national organizations as well as local programs you can turn to for help filling the shelves of your library, classroom, or literacy program and putting books into the hands and homes of young readers.

Schools Outreach

We have provided brand new textbooks to Stony Brook High School, Middleburg Middle School, Caltra Middle School, Weeping Willow Elementary and more.

Familial Specific Outreach Through Edu. Defecit Program

Through our recent Education Defecit Program, we have had the pleasure of helping several families in the Chicago area who experience a difficulty of attaining educational materials. The Wilkins and Raterford Families have submitted their stories which will be soon accessible on our webpage.
